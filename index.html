The Core Technology

Avalanche's high performance is not accidental. It's the result of a unique multi-chain architecture and a novel consensus protocol. This design allows for specialization, preventing the congestion that plagues monolithic blockchains.

Subnets: The Secret to Infinite Scalability

Subnets (Subnetworks) are sovereign networks that define their own rules for membership and tokenomics. They allow projects to have their own dedicated, custom blockchains, preventing them from competing for resources with other dApps on the main network. This is how Avalanche achieves horizontal scaling.
